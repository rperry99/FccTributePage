Who is she?

Cassandra Peterson is an American actress from Manhattan, Kansas. Probably the biggest thing she is known for is her horror hostess character Elvira. One of her mian roles as Elvira is her show called Elvira's Movie Macabre where Elvira is a hostess for different horror-themed movies. Her character would make apperarances during the show and make comments on what was going on or poke fun at the the silly plots of the movies.

Cassandra Peterson also acted in some movies as well. There are several "Elvira" movies where Elvira is the star. She has made other movie appearances as other characters such as a biker boss in Pee-Wee's Big Adventure.

  • The Dunwich Horror (1970)
  • Curse of the Crimson Alter (1986)
