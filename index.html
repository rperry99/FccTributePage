Cassandra Peterson

Who is she?

Cassandra Peterson is an American actress from Manhattan, Kansas. One of the most iconic roles she plays is a horror-hostess by the name Elvira. While acting as Elvira in her show Elvira's Movie Macabre, she is the host of many horror-themed movies. Elvira would then appear during the show to add comments that poke fun at what is happening. She might also do a little skit that goes along with the movie before and after commercial breaks.

Cassandra Peterson also acted in some movies as well. There are several "Elvira" movies where Elvira is the star. She has made other movie appearances as other characters such as a biker boss in Pee-Wee's Big Adventure.
