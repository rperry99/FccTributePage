Who is she?

Cassandra Peterson is an American acrtress from MAnhattan, Kansas. Probably the biggest role she is known for is for her horro hostess character Elvira. As Elvira, she has played in many roles in movies but probably her most well known was her TV show as a movie host. The show was called Elvira's Movie Macabre where Elvira is a hostess for many different horror movies. She would interupt the movies with comments about the actors or make jokes about what is going on in the movies.
